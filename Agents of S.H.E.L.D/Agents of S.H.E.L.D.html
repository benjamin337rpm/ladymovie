Agentes da S.H.I.E.L.D.

Following his role in the blockbuster The Avengers, Agent Phil Coulson returns to S.H.I.E.L.D., the law enforcement organization. There, he assembles a small, highly trained group of agents to deal with the strange and the unknown, in cases that have yet to be classified. The team consists of focused agent Grant Ward, an expert in combat and espionage; Agent Melinda May, a pilot and martial arts expert; Agent Leo Fitz, a brilliant scientist if a bit socially out of place; and Agent Jemma Simmons. They will be aided by new civilian recruit Skye, known for her expertise as a computer hacker. Get ready for an epic adventure that highlights the hope and miracle that is the human spirit. This is a world of superheroes and aliens, punctuated by the unusual in spectacular action and stories that span the globe. However, the series speaks to the human condition through the lens of our depowered agents of S.H.I.E.L.D., highlighting that together we are stronger than apart and that we can make a difference in the world.
