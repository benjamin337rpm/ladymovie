jungle cruise

Jungle Cruise revolves around the trickster and prankster Frank Wolff (Dwayne Johnson), captain of the boat La Guilla. He is hired by Dr. Lily Houghton (Emily Blunt) and her brother McGregor (Jack Whitehall) to take them on a mission through the dense Amazon rainforests with the intention of finding a mysterious tree with healing powers that could change the future of medicine forever. On the way, they will experience various dangers, facing wild animals and even supernatural forces.
