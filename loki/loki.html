Loki

Based on the famous villain of the Marvel Cinematic Universe, Loki is a Disney+ original series that takes place after the events narrated in Avengers: Ultimatum. The spin-off follows in the footsteps of Loki (Tom Hiddleston), better known as the God of Mischief, who managed to steal the tesseract from the Avengers during their mission to retrieve the Infinity Stones. With the power of the space gem, the Asgardian begins to jump through time with the intention of reaching Midgard. Along his journey, he ends up interfering in important moments in human history – either to fulfill his own goals, or to have a little fun. What he doesn't know is that his intervention could generate a catastrophe in the timelines and, thus, jeopardize the entire universe.
